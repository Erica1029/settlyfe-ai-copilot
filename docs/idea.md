# Settlyfe AI Copilot Idea

## 1. Project Name

Settlyfe AI Copilot

## 2. Product Positioning

Settlyfe AI Copilot is an AI-assisted rental planning feature embedded inside the existing Settlyfe rental app.

It is not a standalone AI product. It helps users clarify rental strategy before browsing listings or applying for housing.

## 3. First Validation Market

V1 focuses on San Diego / UCSD / North America student rental.

This market fits Settlyfe's current North American student and young-renter housing direction.

The product logic should remain extensible to other high-mobility rental markets such as Shanghai, Hong Kong, New York, and other student / intern relocation cities.

## 4. Target Users

- International students moving to a new city.
- Interns relocating for a short-term role.
- Young renters making their first independent rental decision.
- Users who feel overwhelmed before they know what to search for.

## 5. Problem

Most rental platforms start with listings. Many young renters are not ready for listings yet.

They need to clarify budget, commute, area type, room type, furniture needs, lifestyle tradeoffs, and move-in preparation before they search.

## 6. Why Traditional Rental Platforms Do Not Solve This

Listing platforms are optimized for browsing inventory.

They usually do not help users build a rental strategy before search. They also do not turn vague preferences into explainable recommendations, risks, next steps, and moving checklists.

## 7. AI Value

Settlyfe AI Copilot turns structured user preferences into a rental plan.

In V1, AI behavior is simulated using structured mock data and rule-based recommendation logic.

The output should feel explainable:

- Best Fit Area / Example Match.
- Why this fits.
- Watch out for.
- Other Good Matches.
- Next steps.
- Moving Checklist.

V2 can upgrade the explanation layer with a real AI API.

## 8. V1 MVP

- Interactive mock-data product demo.
- Home with AI Copilot banner inside the original Settlyfe homepage.
- 3-step preference input:
  - Move Basics.
  - Housing Needs.
  - Lifestyle & Priorities.
- AI Loading.
- AI Result Plan.
- Moving Checklist.
- Structured San Diego mock rental / neighborhood data.
- Local state and rule-based recommendation engine.
- No real AI API, no live rental API, no map API, no database, no login, no payment.

## 9. Future Versions

### V2: AI-Powered Recommendation Explanation

Use a real AI API to generate recommendation explanations, watch-outs, next steps, and checklist language while keeping rental data controlled.

### V3: Data-Connected City MVP

Start with San Diego as the first validation city, then extend to other high-mobility rental markets such as Shanghai, Hong Kong, New York, and other student / intern relocation cities.
